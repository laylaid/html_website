This is a high speed racing game

The point of the game is to avoid the traffic to win

Users can pick what color car they would like to race with and what level (easy, medium, hard)

When the game starts users can move the car using the arrow keys, as the game goes on, cars appear to act as obstacles

If a player can manage to avoid the cars for the specified amount of time(based on which level was chosen), a you win screen will appear

This is Fruit Ninja

The point of the game is to click the most amount of fruits in 30 seconds while avoiding the bombs

Users input their name which is later used in a leaderboard at the end of the game

When the game starts, the player moves the ninja to hit the fruits while avoiding the bombs

They are given 30 seconds to click as many fruits and if they hit a bomb the game automatically ends

Once the game is over, it displays the players name and its final score


This is Make your own drink

Users are prompted to input their name and they see a Hello!

Players can make their own drink with a variety of options that the game provides

They can choose which cup they like, what type of drink, if they want ice, whipped cream, and what type of background

Music plays in the background and stars can be seen moving

Once players finish they press the done button and the game removes all the options and displays just the drink and its name with its background
